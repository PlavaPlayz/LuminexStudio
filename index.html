About Luminex Studio

Short:
Luminex Studio is a studio built by a group of friends aiming to create the best future games possible across multiple platforms, including Virtual Reality and Roblox.

Long:
Luminex Studio is a Virtual Reality and Roblox company that has been creating games and pushing them toward the top of the industry. Founded by Vision and Slipz at the young ages of 14 and 15, the studio began with a passion for VR and evolved into a full creative studio. Today, Luminex Studio focuses on building future‑ready games driven by creativity, fun, and strong marketing.

What We Do

Luminex Studios develops immersive Roblox and VR (Meta Quest) games, blending gameplay innovation with striking visuals. Our projects focus on fun, immersion, and next‑level player experiences.

Our Vision

Our vision is to push boundaries in both Roblox and VR gaming. With our first major game nearing release, we aim to deliver unforgettable experiences that players can truly step into.
