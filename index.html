About Luminex Studio

Short:
Luminex Studio is a studio built by a group of friends aiming to create the best future games possible across Virtual Reality and Roblox.

Long:
Luminex Studio is a VR and Roblox company founded by Vision and Slipz at the ages of 14 and 15. What started as a passion project evolved into a full studio focused on creativity, innovation, and unforgettable player experiences.

What We Do

We develop immersive Roblox and VR (Meta Quest) games, blending unique mechanics, creative worlds, and strong community-driven design.

Our Vision

With our first major games nearing release, our mission is to push boundaries, stand out in the industry, and deliver fun, memorable experiences across platforms.
